SYNOPSIS

In the shadows of a forgotten chapel deep in rural Massachusetts, a dark ritual was performed — one that would echo across generations. In 1976, renowned paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren were called to uncover a sinister force unlike any they had ever encountered. What began as a routine exorcism unraveled into a terrifying descent into the heart of ancient evil. This is the story of The Rites — a case so disturbing, it was kept hidden… until now.
