Spooky Ghost Cupcakes

Rich chocolate cupcakes topped with fluffy vanilla frosting ghosts

These ghostly Halloween cupcakes are as spooky as they are delicious. Each treat features a moist rich chocolate base topped with fluffy swirls of vanilla frosting shaped like playful ghosts. Their simple yet festive design makes them perfect to halloween parties.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup of all-pupose flower
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 6 tbsp of cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla extract
For the frosting
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 3 cups of powered sugar
  • 2 tbsp of vanilla extract
  • chocolate chips for decor

Steps

Cupcake mix
  1. Preheat oven to 350° F and prepare a cupcake tray with cupcake liners
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and salt
  3. Add egg, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  4. Fill cupcake liners half way and bake for 15 to 20 minutes
Frosting
  1. Beat butter until creamy (about 5 to 7 minutes)
  2. Gradually add powered sugar, then vanilla extract and mix until fluffly
  3. Pipe tall swirls on each cupcake to form the ghost shapes
  4. Add two chocolate chips as eyes to complete the look
